On the basis of these data and our recent study (dubbed “Discovery”), we think that we are beginning to understand the dynamics of the developmental processes for this novel yeast housing and ultimately may lead to a more elaborate view of how a cell maintains its biofunctional functionality and in turn, maybe a better understanding of disorders where lipids get elevated, for example, neurodegenerative disease.
