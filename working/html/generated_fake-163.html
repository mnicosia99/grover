The genus NEOLOBVUS is a cousin of dengue and similar to roundworm, and also closely related to Japanese tsukake. The WHO has expanded their definition of this disease and now it is defined as NEOLOBVUS Type 1 pneumophilia. Results have been published in the European Journal of Microbiology.
