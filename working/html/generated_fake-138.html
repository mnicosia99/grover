This article summarizes CTX-M-1 in vivo findings of an extensive study. PET/CTX and experiments with CTX-M-1 producing Klebsiella pneumoniae and mode of degradation and development of forms of Carbapenem resistant Klebsiella in the gut provides sufficient data to define the role of K2 in both pathogenesis and apoptosis in antibiotic resistance.
